A solution to URI's parking problem. Made for students, by students.

EasyParking is currently undergoing internal testing. As such, the app is not yet available for download. Please register to receive updates soon.

Features

Live Occupancy

Get real-time updates on the availability of parking spaces across various lots. Easily find out which locations are busy or open to park.

Instant Feedback

Share your observations about parking lot availability with the community in real time, helping others make informed decisions.

Parking Services

Access URI's parking services directly through the app. Pay tickets, buy parking passes, and manage all related services with ease.

Spot Saver

Save the exact location of your parked car for future reference. Never forget where you parked again, even in the busiest lots.

Leaving Notification

Notify other users when you're leaving a parking spot. This feature helps fellow drivers find available spaces quickly.

Parking History

Review your parking history, including locations, arrival times, and departure times. Easily track where you’ve parked and for how long.
